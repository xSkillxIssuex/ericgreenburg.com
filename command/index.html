The Ritual (this IS the app)

Morning — 5 minutes

Open Claude Code in this directory. First pull Apple Notes:

python3 scripts/pull_apple_notes.py

Then say: "morning".

Claude reads INBOX.md, TODAY.md, LOOPS.md, LEDGER.md, asks what's on the calendar, and gives you the day: what must happen, what's slipping, what to ignore. You confirm or edit TODAY.md. Done.

Capture — all day, zero friction

Apple Notes folder: "Command Surface Inbox". Dump everything there — tasks, ideas, worries, half-thoughts. No taxonomy, no tags, no discipline required at capture time.

Pull Notes into this surface:

python3 scripts/pull_apple_notes.py
python3 scripts/scrape_notes_todos.py

The first command appends new notes from Command Surface Inbox into INBOX.md, keeps a raw archive in imports/apple-notes/, and does not edit or delete anything in Notes. The second command scans notes modified in the last 1 day and writes possible actions to TODO_CANDIDATES.md.

One-time baseline command:

python3 scripts/scrape_notes_todos.py --days 30

After the baseline, do not poll deeper than 1 day unless you explicitly ask for a deeper review.

Evening — 2 minutes (optional but compounding)

Run python3 scripts/pull_apple_notes.py and python3 scripts/scrape_notes_todos.py. Say: "triage". Claude sorts each item into TODAY / LOOPS / LEDGER-candidate / ignore, and empties the inbox.

Friday — 15 minutes

Say: "weekly review". Claude walks LOOPS.md (close, kill, or promote anything stale), checks TODAY completion honestly, appends any new decisions to LEDGER.md, and drafts Monday's TODAY.md.

Rules

No new content files. Four files + inbox are the operating surface. Scripts and raw import archives exist only to pull Apple Notes into the surface.

No app until earned. The web-app build (spec archived in LEDGER) unlocks after 90 consecutive days of this ritual AND the 2026-07-08 TODAY list fully done. Building it becomes the reward for shipping, not the substitute.

The ledger is append-only. Decisions get revised by new dated entries, never by editing history.

When the system feels inadequate, that's a feeling, not a requirement. Log it in LOOPS. It will pass, or it will earn the build.
